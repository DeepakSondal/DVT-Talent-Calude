# DVT Talent AI — 15-Minute Executive Demo Script

**Objective**: Demonstrate the transition from manual, high-friction recruiting to autonomous, high-precision swarm intelligence.

---

## 00:00 - 02:00 // The Problem & Nexus Hook
- **Scene**: Log into the Dashboard.
- **Talking Point**: "Most recruiting teams are drowning in manual filters and stale data. Welcome to The Nexus—your 24/7 window into a recruitment swarm that never sleeps."
- **Action**: Highlight the live activity feed. "Every signal you see here is an agent actively discovering, validating, and engaging talent in real-time."

## 02:00 - 05:00 // Swarm Ignition (The Sourcing Hub)
- **Scene**: Navigate to `/dashboard/sourcing`.
- **Talking Point**: "We don't search; we discover. Let's initiate a swarm for Generative AI talent in Silicon Valley."
- **Action**: Select sectors and click 'Initiate Swarm Discovery'. Transition back to The Nexus to watch the agents "wake up."

## 05:00 - 08:00 // Intelligence Synthesis (Candidates)
- **Scene**: Open the Candidate Grid.
- **Talking Point**: "The swarm has returned our first batch of nodes. Notice the Integrity Score. Our agents don't just find names; they validate technical authenticity."
- **Action**: Click a candidate, show the Integrity Scorecard and the AI Synthesis notes.

## 08:00 - 11:00 // Ethical Outreach (Composer)
- **Scene**: Open the Outreach Page.
- **Talking Point**: "Autonomous doesn't mean irresponsible. When we engage, we do it with integrity."
- **Action**: Type 'We need a ninja programmer' in the composer. Show the **Bias Guardrail** alerting the user. "The platform ensures your brand remains inclusive and professional at scale."

## 11:00 - 15:00 // Governance & Close
- **Scene**: Audit Log & Analytics.
- **Talking Point**: "For our enterprise partners, transparency is paramount. Every action the AI takes is logged here, verifiable and audit-ready."
- **Action**: Quick scroll of the Audit Log.
- **Closing**: "DVT Talent AI isn't just a tool; it's a workforce multiplier. Questions?"
